Each AI agent appears as a **planet** with a unique visual style based on its type.

- Click a planet to select it and see its metrics in the Command Center
- Click the black hole at the center to see cosmic statistics
- Click empty space to spawn astronauts that drift through the universe
- Watch for **ships** flying between planets (agents sharing a workspace) and **lightning arcs** when agents edit the same file

Use **Demo** mode (control grid) to see the universe populated with simulated agents.
