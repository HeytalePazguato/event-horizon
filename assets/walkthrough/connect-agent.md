Click the **Connect** button in the Command Center (bottom-right control grid) to open the connection wizard.

Choose your agent and click **Install**:

- **Claude Code** — adds hooks to `~/.claude/settings.json`
- **OpenCode** — installs a plugin to `~/.config/opencode/plugins/`
- **GitHub Copilot** — installs hooks to `.github/hooks/`

Start an agent session and its planet will appear automatically in the universe.
